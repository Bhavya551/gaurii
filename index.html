Who I Am

An explorer at the intersection of worlds

I am a second-year MBBS student at Chhattisgarh Institute of Medical Sciences, Bilaspur, driven by a deep passion for the science of healing and the art of human connection.

Alongside my medical journey, I am pursuing a certified BBA in Digital Business & Entrepreneurship from IIM Bangalore, because I believe the future of healthcare will be shaped by those who can think like clinicians and lead like entrepreneurs.

Beyond academics, I am a national-award-winning Kathak performer, a content creator, an avid baker, and someone who finds beauty in both the precise and the spontaneous.
